About

Background, expertise and the next chapter

Then

Started in freelance media relations and brand work, then federal technology and defense PR at Sage Communications, then two roles at Transurban North America, a global toll road operator: corporate communications, followed by brand marketing, with full ownership of the company's social channels alongside newsroom writing, video production and campaign development.

Now

Director of Marketing and Brand at the Virginia Chamber of Commerce, managing brand, advocacy and member communications for an organization that operates as both a nonprofit and a business advocacy group. The role spans legislative one-pagers and press releases along with the Chamber's website, email programs and full social media presence.

Next

Finishing up Master of Business Administration at William & Mary. Watching something that requires zero critical thinking. Recommendations welcome.
